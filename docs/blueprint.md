# **App Name**: Federalismo Portal

## Core Features:

- Onboarding & Kartilya Agreement: Guides new users through the party's fundamental principles (Kartilya) with a scrollable card and requires agreement ('I Agree / Nananalig Ako') before full app access.
- Personalized Member Dashboard: Displays the user's name, role, and jurisdiction level, along with quick summary statistics (Verified Members, Pending Approvals, Upcoming Events) and recent announcements.
- Dynamic Organizational Directory: Provides a filterable view of party officers at national, regional, provincial, city/municipal, and barangay levels with individual cards including placeholder names and avatars for 13 key roles.
- Program Agenda Explorer: Presents the party's core pillars as clickable cards (e.g., Constitutional Reform, Economics, Civil Government & Security), revealing detailed bullet points for each section.
- AI-Powered Announcement Summaries: Utilizes an AI tool to automatically generate concise briefings and summaries of longer party announcements or policy documents for quick member consumption on the dashboard.
- Mobile-First Responsive Navigation: Implements a global navigation system with a bottom navigation bar for mobile devices and a responsive side drawer for larger screens, offering quick access to Home, Directory, Agendas, and Profile.
- Member Overview Statistics: Displays critical summary metrics such as 'Total Verified Members' and 'Pending Approvals' on the dashboard, providing an at-a-glance overview of membership status.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, patriotic Navy Blue (#194278), symbolizing strength, professionalism, and integrity. Used for major calls to action and primary branding.
- Secondary color: Strong, impactful Deep Red (#8A0F0F), representing passion and the patriotic spirit, used for secondary elements or highlights that require attention.
- Accent color: Vibrant Golden Yellow (#F9CB0D), evoking pride and prominence, strategically applied to interactive elements, icons, or specific emphasized details.
- Background color: A clean, neutral Off-white/Light Gray (#EFF1F3), providing a light and professional canvas that allows content to stand out clearly.
- Both headline and body text will use 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its clean, modern, and highly legible appearance, suitable for a professional and objective presentation of information across all screen sizes.
- Utilize clear, symbolic icons for navigation and content representation. For instance, the 'Agendas' tab will use icons such as Building/Pillars for Constitutional Reform, Gavel for Politics, and a Shield for Civil Government & Security, to enhance immediate comprehension.
- A mobile-first, responsive design approach, featuring a global bottom navigation bar on mobile and a flexible side drawer on larger screens. Content will be presented using standard UI components like Cards for statistics and officer profiles, and ListTiles for feeds and detailed agendas, ensuring clarity and intuitive interaction.
- Incorporate subtle and professional animations for navigation transitions, card expansions, and filter changes to enhance user experience without distraction, maintaining a refined aesthetic.
